Next Steps to Complete Setup

  1. Generate the PNG files using the icon generator tool: Open Icon Generator
  2. Save each generated PNG image to the correct location in the /icons/ folder
  3. Uncomment the PNG icon references in index.html
  4. Uncomment the PNG icon references in service-worker.js
  5. Build and deploy your app
Note: The SVG icon will work for most modern browsers, but PNG files are needed for complete compatibility with all devices, especially older browsers and some mobile platforms.

Testing Your Icon on Mobile Devices

After you've completed the setup and deployed your app:

  • iOS: Open Safari, browse to your app, tap the Share button, then "Add to Home Screen"
  • Android: Open Chrome, browse to your app, tap the menu (⋮), then "Add to Home screen"
